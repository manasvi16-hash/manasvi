About Me

Hi, I’m Manasvi Kumbhar, a second-year Communication Design student with a passion for storytelling through visuals. My work explores diverse design disciplines, including branding, typography, animation, and packaging. I love combining creativity with technical expertise to bring ideas to life. I’ve had the privilege of working on various creative projects, such as: Designing logos, posters, and labels inspired by historical art movements like Baroque, Romanticism, and Impressionism. Crafting unique packaging concepts, like a clapper box for evergreen hits. Experimenting with stop-motion animation, including a project on chaos theory. Researching how phone usage impacts self-esteem among working-age individuals in India. When I’m not designing, you’ll often find me singing, playing the guitar, or enjoying a game of badminton. Through my portfolio, I aim to share my creative journey and highlight the thought process behind each project.

My Projects

MANDALA ART

This artwork beautifully combines mandala art, known for its meditative and calming properties, with a Hindu cultural theme. The use of bright and contrasting colors symbolizes vitality, devotion, and spirituality. The peacock feather designs further enhance the connection to Lord Krishna, as peacocks hold special significance in his lore. This piece serves as both a decorative and spiritual artwork, ideal for: Home décor, especially in meditation or prayer rooms. Gifting, as it resonates with cultural and spiritual values. Exhibiting artistic craftsmanship with a deep cultural connection.

RESIN ART

These resin trays are versatile pieces that combine utility and artistry. They can be used for serving drinks, displaying accessories, or simply as decorative items to enhance home interiors. The resin's durability and high-gloss finish ensure these trays are both practical and visually appealing.These trays are perfect for those who appreciate handcrafted, artistic homeware and make excellent gifts or statement pieces for any space.

TYPOGRAPHY POSTER

The guitar-shaped text arrangement is the highlight of the poster. It cleverly uses words associated with music, such as "guitar," "rhythm," "melody," and "bass," to visually construct the shape of a guitar. This typographic artwork adds an engaging and thematic touch. Along the left edge, a vertical typographic design features words like "jazz," "play," and "sing," emphasizing diverse aspects of music and culture.
